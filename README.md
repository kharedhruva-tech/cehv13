## 📌 About CEH v13 AI
The CEH v13 is the industry-leading ethical hacking credential, now updated to integrate Artificial Intelligence across the 5 traditional phases of ethical hacking. It validates proficiency across 20 modules, over 550 distinct attack techniques, and 4,000+ commercial-grade security tools.

### Exam Blueprint
*   **Exam Code:** 312-50v13
*   **Format:** 125 Multiple Choice Questions
*   **Duration:** 4 Hours
*   **Passing Score:** 60% to 85% (Variable depending on question form)
*   **Core Pillars:** Learn ➡️ Certify ➡️ Engage ➡️ Compete
